Pistols

Handguns designed for self-defense, law enforcement, and military sidearm use.

Glock 17
AUSTRIA SEMI-AUTO 9x19mm

The world's most popular polymer-framed service pistol.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 625g
  • Length: 204mm
  • Action: Safe Action
HISTORY & SERVICE

Introduced in 1982, the Glock 17 revolutionized the handgun market with its "Safe Action" system and high-capacity polymer frame. It is the standard sidearm for law enforcement agencies worldwide, including the FBI and British Armed Forces.

Beretta M9
ITALY SEMI-AUTO 9x19mm

The standard US military sidearm for over 30 years.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 952g
  • Length: 217mm
  • Action: DA/SA
HISTORY & SERVICE

The M9 replaced the M1911 in 1985 as the primary pistol of the US Armed Forces. Known for its open-slide design which reduces stove-pipe jams, it has seen action in every major US conflict since the Gulf War.

SIG Sauer P226
GERMANY SEMI-AUTO 9x19mm

A precision instrument favored by Navy SEALs and elite units.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 964g
  • Length: 196mm
  • Frame: Aluminum Alloy
HISTORY & SERVICE

The P226 was the runner-up in the US trials against the Beretta M9 but was adopted by the Navy SEALs (Mk25) for its superior corrosion resistance and accuracy. It features a decocker instead of a safety.

Colt M1911
USA SEMI-AUTO .45 ACP

The legendary .45 caliber pistol that served in two World Wars.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 1100g
  • Length: 210mm
  • Action: Single Action
HISTORY & SERVICE

Designed by John Browning, the M1911 is famous for its crisp single-action trigger and the stopping power of the .45 ACP round. It remains a favorite among custom gunsmiths and special operations units (MEU(SOC)).

CZ 75
CZECH SEMI-AUTO 9x19mm

Renowned for its low bore axis and exceptional ergonomics.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 1120g
  • Length: 206mm
  • Feature: Internal Rails
HISTORY & SERVICE

The CZ 75 features internal slide rails (the slide rides inside the frame), which lowers the bore axis and reduces muzzle flip. It is one of the most copied pistol designs in the world, used extensively by police forces.

FN Five-seveN
BELGIUM SEMI-AUTO 5.7x28mm

High-tech pistol designed to defeat body armor.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 100m
  • Weight: 610g
  • Length: 208mm
  • Ammo: 5.7x28mm
HISTORY & SERVICE

Designed to companion the P90 SMG, the Five-seveN fires a unique high-velocity round capable of penetrating Kevlar vests. Its large magazine capacity and extremely light weight make it a favorite for tactical teams.

Walther P99
GERMANY SEMI-AUTO 9x19mm

Famous as James Bond's modern sidearm of choice.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 630g
  • Length: 180mm
  • Feature: AS Trigger
HISTORY & SERVICE

The P99 introduced the "Anti-Stress" trigger system and interchangeable backstraps for custom grip sizing. It gained fame in pop culture as the weapon of choice for Pierce Brosnan's James Bond.

Tokarev TT-33
RUSSIA SEMI-AUTO 7.62x25mm

The rugged Soviet workhorse of World War II.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 854g
  • Length: 194mm
  • Action: Single Action
HISTORY & SERVICE

Based on the Browning design but simplified for mass production, the TT-33 fires a very high-velocity cartridge capable of penetrating thick winter clothing and light cover. It lacks a manual safety, reflecting Soviet doctrine.

Desert Eagle
USA/ISRAEL MAGNUM .50 AE

The heavy hitter of the pistol world, famous for pure power.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 200m
  • Weight: 2000g
  • Length: 273mm
  • Op: Gas-Operated
HISTORY & SERVICE

Unlike most pistols, the Desert Eagle is gas-operated, similar to a rifle. While impractical for combat due to its weight and low capacity, its ability to fire the massive .50 AE round makes it a cultural icon.

Browning Hi-Power
BELGIUM SEMI-AUTO 9x19mm

The "King of Nines" - the first high-capacity wonder nine.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 1000g
  • Length: 197mm
  • Mag: 13 Rounds
HISTORY & SERVICE

John Browning's final design, completed by Dieudonné Saive. It was the first widely used pistol with a double-stack magazine (13 rounds), setting the standard for all modern "Wonder Nines" that followed.
