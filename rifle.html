Assault Rifles

The standard infantry weapon. Balanced for range, accuracy, and volume of fire.

AK-47
RUSSIA ASSAULT RIFLE 7.62x39mm

The most widely produced and recognizable firearm in history.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 300m
  • Weight: 3.47 kg
  • Length: 880mm
  • Action: Gas-operated
HISTORY & SERVICE

Designed by Mikhail Kalashnikov in 1947, the AK-47 is legendary for its ability to function in mud, sand, and water. Its loose tolerances make it less accurate than Western rifles, but infinitely more reliable in harsh conditions.

M4 Carbine
USA CARBINE 5.56 NATO

The versatile standard-issue rifle of the US Military.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 500m
  • Weight: 2.88 kg
  • Length: 840mm
  • Rails: Picatinny
HISTORY & SERVICE

A shorter, lighter evolution of the M16A2, the M4 Carbine excels in Close Quarters Battle (CQB). Its extensive rail system allows soldiers to mount optics, lasers, and grenade launchers, making it the most modular rifle in service.

FN SCAR-L
BELGIUM ASSAULT RIFLE 5.56 NATO

Special Operations Forces Combat Assault Rifle - Light.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 600m
  • Weight: 3.29 kg
  • Length: 850mm
  • Stock: Folding
HISTORY & SERVICE

Developed for USSOCOM, the SCAR features a monolithic upper receiver and polymer lower. It is known for its low recoil impulse and adaptability, though it is significantly more expensive than the M4 platform.

H&K G36
GERMANY ASSAULT RIFLE 5.56 NATO

Advanced polymer rifle with integrated optical sights.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 800m
  • Weight: 3.63 kg
  • Length: 999mm
  • Material: Polymer
HISTORY & SERVICE

The G36 replaced the G3 in the German Bundeswehr. It uses extensive fiber-reinforced polymer to save weight and includes a built-in 3x optical scope in the carry handle, a revolutionary feature at its release.

FAMAS F1
FRANCE BULLPUP 5.56 NATO

"Le Clairon" (The Bugle) - France's iconic bullpup rifle.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 450m
  • Weight: 3.61 kg
  • Length: 757mm
  • Action: Lever-delayed
HISTORY & SERVICE

The FAMAS features a bullpup layout, where the magazine is behind the trigger. This allows for a full-length barrel in a compact weapon. It is famous for its extremely high rate of fire for an assault rifle.

Steyr AUG A3
AUSTRIA BULLPUP 5.56 NATO

A futuristic modular weapon system.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 500m
  • Weight: 3.6 kg
  • Length: 790mm
  • Feature: Swappable Barrel
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Armee-Universal-Gewehr (AUG) was the first commercially successful bullpup. Its modular design allows a soldier to swap barrels in seconds, converting it from a carbine to a light machine gun.

IWI Tavor TAR-21
ISRAEL BULLPUP 5.56 NATO

Designed specifically for urban combat (CQB).

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 550m
  • Weight: 3.27 kg
  • Length: 720mm
  • Action: Long-stroke Piston
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Tavor Assault Rifle - 21st Century (TAR-21) replaced the M16 in IDF service. It was designed from the ground up for soldiers exiting armored vehicles and clearing buildings in tight urban environments.

QBZ-95
CHINA BULLPUP 5.8x42mm

The standard service rifle of the People's Liberation Army.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 400m
  • Weight: 3.25 kg
  • Length: 745mm
  • Ammo: DBP87
HISTORY & SERVICE

The QBZ-95 fires a unique Chinese 5.8mm cartridge, claimed to have better ballistics than both NATO 5.56 and Soviet 5.45. It features a polymer construction and is strictly used by Chinese forces.

SA80 / L85A2
UK BULLPUP 5.56 NATO

The troubled British rifle redeemed by German engineering.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 500m
  • Weight: 3.82 kg
  • Length: 785mm
  • Optic: SUSAT / ACOG
HISTORY & SERVICE

The original L85A1 was notorious for jamming. Heckler & Koch (then British-owned) completely rebuilt the internal mechanism to create the A2 variant, which is now considered one of the most accurate service rifles in the world.

Galil ACE
ISRAEL ASSAULT RIFLE 7.62x39mm

A modernized, ergonomic evolution of the AK platform.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 400m
  • Weight: 3.4 kg
  • Length: 875mm
  • Action: Long-stroke Gas
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Galil ACE takes the legendary reliability of the original Galil (and by extension, the AK-47) and adds modern polymer weight savings, Picatinny rails, and left-side charging handles. It is popular in South America and Vietnam.
