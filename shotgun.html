Shotguns

Close-quarters firearms designed for maximum impact and versatility.

Remington 870
USA PUMP ACTION 12 GAUGE

The most widely used pump-action shotgun in history.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 40m (Buck)
  • Weight: 3.6 kg
  • Length: 970mm
  • Receiver: Steel
HISTORY & SERVICE

Introduced in 1950, the Remington 870 features a milled steel receiver, making it incredibly tough. With over 11 million built, it serves in police cruisers, military units, and hunting blinds worldwide.

Benelli M4
ITALY SEMI-AUTO 12 GAUGE

The US Marine Corps standard combat shotgun.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 3.82 kg
  • Length: 885mm
  • System: ARGO Gas
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Benelli M4 uses the patented ARGO (Auto-Regulating Gas Operated) system, which self-cleans and adjusts for different loads. It won the US military shotgun trials for its ability to function without maintenance in desert conditions.

Saiga-12
RUSSIA MAG-FED 12 GAUGE

An AK-47 chambered for 12-gauge shotgun shells.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 40m
  • Weight: 3.6 kg
  • Length: 1145mm
  • Feed: Box/Drum Mag
HISTORY & SERVICE

By adapting the Kalashnikov long-stroke gas system, the Saiga-12 brings assault rifle reload speeds to the shotgun world. It accepts 5, 8, 10, or even 20-round drum magazines, offering devastating firepower.

Mossberg 500
USA PUMP ACTION 12 GAUGE

Rugged, affordable, and ambidextrous workhorse.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 40m
  • Weight: 3.4 kg
  • Length: Varied
  • Safety: Top-mounted
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Mossberg 500 is known for its "loose" tolerances, which allow it to function even when full of grit. It is unique for its top-mounted safety, making it accessible for both left and right-handed shooters.

Winchester 1897
USA HISTORIC 12 GAUGE

The "Trench Broom" that terrified enemies in WWI.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 30m
  • Weight: 3.6 kg
  • Length: 1000mm
  • Feature: Slam Fire
HISTORY & SERVICE

Designed by John Browning, the M1897 could be "slam fired"—holding the trigger down and pumping the action rapidly. It was so effective in WWI trenches that Germany attempted to have it banned as a war crime.

Franchi SPAS-12
ITALY DUAL MODE 12 GAUGE

Iconic combat shotgun with selectable pump/auto modes.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 40m
  • Weight: 4.4 kg
  • Length: 1041mm
  • Stock: Folding Hook
HISTORY & SERVICE

The SPAS-12 (Special Purpose Automatic Shotgun) allows the user to switch between pump-action (for low-power rounds) and semi-auto (for full power buckshot) at the press of a button. Famous for its menacing look in cinema.

Kel-Tec KSG
USA BULLPUP 12 GAUGE

Dual-tube bullpup shotgun with massive capacity.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 40m
  • Weight: 3.1 kg
  • Length: 663mm
  • Feed: Dual Tube
HISTORY & SERVICE

The KSG features two magazine tubes under the barrel, which the user can switch between manually. This allows for a total of 14 rounds of capacity in a package shorter than a standard M4 carbine.

Atchisson AA-12
USA FULL AUTO 12 GAUGE

Fully automatic, low-recoil combat shotgun.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 7.3 kg
  • Length: 966mm
  • Feed: Drum Mag
HISTORY & SERVICE

The AA-12 uses a proprietary "Constant Recoil" system that reduces felt recoil by almost 90%, allowing it to be fired fully automatic with one hand. It is capable of firing explosive FRAG-12 rounds.

Ithaca 37
USA PUMP ACTION 12 GAUGE

Bottom-ejecting shotgun favored by police.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 40m
  • Weight: 3.0 kg
  • Length: Varied
  • Eject: Bottom
HISTORY & SERVICE

Based on a Remington Model 17 patent, the Ithaca 37 loads and ejects from the bottom. This seals the action against dirt and makes it truly ambidextrous. It was the standard LAPD shotgun for decades.

Stoeger M3000
ITALY/TURKEY SEMI-AUTO 12 GAUGE

Affordable inertia-driven semi-auto.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 40m
  • Weight: 3.3 kg
  • Length: 1170mm
  • System: Inertia
HISTORY & SERVICE

A subsidiary of Benelli, Stoeger uses the same famous Inertia Driven system but at a budget price point. It is widely popular in 3-Gun competitions due to its reliability and ease of modification.
