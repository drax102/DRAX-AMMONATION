Submachine Guns

High-rate-of-fire weapons chambered in pistol calibers for Close Quarters Battle (CQB).

H&K MP5
GERMANY SMG 9x19mm

The gold standard for counter-terrorism units worldwide.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 200m
  • Weight: 2.5 kg
  • Length: 680mm
  • System: Roller-delayed
HISTORY & SERVICE

The MP5 gained worldwide fame after Operation Nimrod (the Iranian Embassy Siege) in 1980. Its closed-bolt, roller-delayed blowback system makes it incredibly accurate for an SMG, minimizing collateral damage.

IMI Uzi
ISRAEL SMG 9x19mm

Rugged, simple, and reliable telescoping-bolt SMG.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 200m
  • Weight: 3.5 kg
  • Length: 470mm
  • Action: Open Bolt
HISTORY & SERVICE

Designed by Major Uziel Gal, the Uzi uses a telescoping bolt that wraps around the barrel, making the weapon extremely compact. It is one of the most widely sold SMGs in history, favored for vehicle crews and security.

FN P90
BELGIUM PDW 5.7x28mm

Futuristic bullpup PDW with high armor penetration.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 200m
  • Weight: 2.6 kg
  • Length: 505mm
  • Feed: Top-mounted
HISTORY & SERVICE

The P90 was created to defeat body armor that standard 9mm rounds could not penetrate. Its unique top-mounted magazine holds 50 rounds, and its bullpup design keeps it incredibly short for vehicle crews.

H&K MP7
GERMANY PDW 4.6x30mm

Ultra-compact PDW designed to pierce Kevlar.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 200m
  • Weight: 1.9 kg
  • Length: 415mm
  • Op: Gas-operated
HISTORY & SERVICE

The MP7 bridges the gap between a machine pistol and a carbine. It uses a scaled-down rifle gas system (like the G36) instead of blowback, making it controllable and accurate despite its tiny size. Favored by SEAL Team 6.

PPSh-41
RUSSIA HISTORIC 7.62x25mm

"Papasha" - The legendary Soviet burp gun of WWII.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 200m
  • Weight: 3.63 kg
  • Length: 843mm
  • Feed: 71 Drum
HISTORY & SERVICE

Produced in the millions during WWII, the PPSh-41 overwhelmed German forces with sheer volume of fire. Its chrome-lined barrel and stamped steel construction made it incredibly robust in the Russian winter.

KRISS Vector
USA SMG .45 ACP

Advanced recoil mitigation system for controllability.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 100m
  • Weight: 2.7 kg
  • Length: 617mm
  • Tech: Super V
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Vector uses the "Super V" system, which directs the bolt's recoil energy downward rather than straight back. This nearly eliminates muzzle climb, allowing accurate fully automatic fire even with heavy .45 ACP rounds.

MAC-10
USA MACHINE PISTOL .45 ACP

"The Phone Booth Gun" - small, cheap, and extremely fast.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 50m
  • Weight: 2.84 kg
  • Length: 269mm
  • Action: Open Bolt
HISTORY & SERVICE

A simple, stamped-steel box that spews bullets. It was designed to be cheap and effective for close-range combat. Often used with a large suppressor that doubles as a foregrip to control its wild recoil.

CZ Scorpion EVO 3
CZECH SMG 9x19mm

Modern polymer SMG with excellent ergonomics.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 200m
  • Weight: 2.77 kg
  • Length: 670mm
  • Mode: Semi/Burst/Auto
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Scorpion EVO 3 is a lightweight, blowback-operated SMG. Its low cost, high reliability, and modularity have made it a hit with police forces and civilian sport shooters worldwide.

Sterling L2A3
UK SMG 9x19mm

British classic known for reliability (and Stormtroopers).

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 200m
  • Weight: 2.7 kg
  • Length: 690mm
  • Mag: Side Mounted
HISTORY & SERVICE

Replacing the Sten gun, the Sterling was built to a much higher standard. Its side-mounted magazine allows the user to get very low to the ground. It famously served as the base prop for the Star Wars E-11 Blaster.

Thompson
USA HISTORIC .45 ACP

"The Chicago Typewriter" - A heavy, rapid-fire icon.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 150m
  • Weight: 4.9 kg
  • Length: 810mm
  • Feed: Stick/Drum
HISTORY & SERVICE

Originally designed for WWI trenches, the Thompson found fame with 1920s gangsters and glory with WWII GIs. It is heavy and expensive to make, but delivers a massive volume of heavy .45 caliber fire.
