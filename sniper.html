Sniper Rifles

Precision long-range firearms designed for high-value target elimination and surveillance.

AWM (L115A3)
UK BOLT ACTION .338 Lapua

The "Super Magnum" known for world-record long-distance kills.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 1500m+
  • Weight: 6.9 kg
  • Length: 1230mm
  • Mag: 5 Rounds
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Arctic Warfare Magnum (AWM) is built to function in extreme freezing conditions. It famously held the world record for the longest confirmed sniper kill (2,475 meters) by Craig Harrison until 2017.

Barrett M82
USA ANTI-MATERIEL .50 BMG

"The Light Fifty" - Designed to stop vehicles and destroy equipment.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 1800m
  • Weight: 14 kg
  • Length: 1400mm
  • Action: Recoil-op
HISTORY & SERVICE

Ronnie Barrett designed this semi-automatic shoulder-fired cannon in his garage. It uses the massive .50 BMG cartridge to punch through engine blocks, radar dishes, and brick walls from over a mile away.

Dragunov SVD
RUSSIA DMR 7.62x54R

The first purpose-built squad designated marksman rifle.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 800m
  • Weight: 4.3 kg
  • Length: 1225mm
  • Action: Gas-operated
HISTORY & SERVICE

Not a true "sniper" rifle by modern Western standards, the SVD was designed to extend the effective range of the infantry squad to 600m+. It is lightweight, rugged, and semi-automatic.

CheyTac M200
USA INTERVENTION .408 CheyTac

Designed for ultra-long-range soft target interdiction.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 2200m+
  • Weight: 14 kg
  • Length: 1346mm
  • System: Bolt Action
HISTORY & SERVICE

The Intervention system includes a ballistic computer to calculate wind and gravity. The .408 round stays supersonic beyond 2000 meters, offering higher accuracy than .50 BMG at extreme distances.

L96A1
UK PRECISION 7.62 NATO

The "Green Meanie" - Predecessor to the mighty AWM.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 800m
  • Weight: 6.5 kg
  • Length: 1180mm
  • Frame: Aluminum
HISTORY & SERVICE

Designed by Olympic shooter Malcolm Cooper, the L96 was the first sniper rifle to use an aluminum chassis system, where the action is bolted to a metal rail rather than bedded in wood or plastic.

M24 SWS
USA SNIPER SYSTEM 7.62 NATO

The US Army's standard Sniper Weapon System for decades.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 800m
  • Weight: 5.4 kg
  • Length: 1092mm
  • Base: Rem 700
HISTORY & SERVICE

Based on the legendary Remington 700 long action, the M24 was designed to be easily convertible to .300 Winchester Magnum. It defines the modern era of US Army sniping.

H&K PSG1
GERMANY POLICE 7.62 NATO

One of the most accurate semi-automatic rifles ever made.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 800m
  • Weight: 7.2 kg
  • Length: 1230mm
  • System: Roller-lock
HISTORY & SERVICE

Developed after the Munich Massacre, the PSG1 (Präzisionsschützengewehr) was designed for police counter-terrorism. It is heavy, expensive, and fragile, but offers bolt-action accuracy with semi-auto speed.

Steyr SSG 08
AUSTRIA MODULAR .338 Lapua

A modern, aluminum-chassis evolution of the SSG 69.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 1500m
  • Weight: 5.7 kg
  • Length: 1182mm
  • Stock: Folding
HISTORY & SERVICE

The SSG 08 was developed with input from Austria's EKO Cobra counter-terror unit. It features a high-tech folding stock and infinite adjustability for the shooter, making it a favorite of elite police units.

Accuracy Int. AXMC
UK MULTI-CAL .338 / .308

The ultimate modular multi-caliber sniper platform.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 1500m
  • Weight: 6.8 kg
  • Length: 1250mm
  • Change: Quick Barrel
HISTORY & SERVICE

The successor to the AWM, the AXMC allows a sniper to change barrels in minutes. They can train with cheap .308 ammo and switch to powerful .338 Lapua for combat missions using the same rifle.

VSS Vintorez
RUSSIA STEALTH 9x39mm

Integrally suppressed special operations sniper rifle.

PERFORMANCE STATS
TECHNICAL SPECS
  • Range: 400m
  • Weight: 2.6 kg
  • Length: 894mm
  • Feature: Subsonic
HISTORY & SERVICE

The VSS uses a heavy, subsonic 9x39mm bullet that can punch through body armor silently. It is the preferred weapon of Russian Spetsnaz for assassination and night raids.
